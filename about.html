Asia Pacific College (APC) and Batang VIP joined forces to spread relevant information about how important aqua life in to our biodiversity, they helped each other by making a website to make their audiences bigger, and to get more attention to potential donator that can save many lives of children in their place and hoping to able to help more children around the Philippines and other countries.

This website is to help promote and share awareness about a non-profit organization called Batang VIP, and their objectives. Batang VIP is about a marine conservation through fisherfolk empowerment and one of their aims is to empower fishing communities in their area, Verde Island Passage. This website aims to promote Batang VIP in different existing social media platforms in order to reach more engagements. It is important to educate children while they are still young so they would know the importance of marine biodiversity and how it affects and impacts our climate, and that's what Batang VIP stands for as well.

Aside from that, the website is about the past, present, and future projects of Batang VIP, and you can also see here how important Batang VIPs mission and vision. And since BatangVIP is currently hosting a fundraiser program the website also has a donation and products page where people might want to go to support them.
